- **Automap pipeline** (VHDL `divmmc.vhd:123-148`):
  - Add `automap_hold_` / `automap_held_` two-stage latches to `DivMmc`.
  - `automap_hold_` = set on M1+MREQ-low at an entry-point PC.
  - `automap_held_` = latched from hold on MREQ rising edge (or scheduled at end of M1).
  - NR 0xBA bit per entry point selects instant (hold→active same cycle) vs delayed (active on the NEXT fetch).

  - Unblocks R3-01..03, IN-04. Also tightens EP-02/EP-03 (currently passing wrongly — check tests flip correctly after the fix).
